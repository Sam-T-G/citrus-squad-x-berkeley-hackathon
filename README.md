# Citrus Squad × Berkeley AI Hackathon 2026

Citrus Squad's entry for the **Berkeley AI Hackathon 2026** at the MLK Jr. Building, UC Berkeley. Hack window opens Saturday June 20 at 10:00 AM and runs 24 hours; full event closes Sunday June 21 at 6:00 PM after judging.

Project direction is fluid until the alignment meeting locks scope. Current frontrunner is **WAND**, a haptic navigation belt for blind and low-vision wearers. Subject to change in the first hour of the hack if the team picks a different idea.

## Where the planning lives

The team playbook is a separate repo, [`citrus-squad-bible`](https://github.com/Sam-T-G/citrus-squad-bible). It holds:

- The ideation board (`ideas/`)
- The 32-hour timeline, pitch craft, judging rubric, sponsor strategy (`docs/`)
- The WAND design documents if WAND remains the chosen idea (`docs/20-` through `docs/24-`)

When the planning changes, the bible changes first. This repo holds whatever code ships.
